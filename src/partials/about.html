I'm Simon Nonnis, a frontend developer and I've been writing code since 2009. I've started building websites for different clients and worked over the years for Ignite, Strike, wi-Q.

I love Javascript and You don’t know JS book series are a fundamental resource into my learning process.

I gained an extensive experience on all phases of the development lifecycle, simplifying the collaboration process between developers, UI designers and project managers to implement a collective vision for a project and deliver stable, fast and high quality websites.

At the end Web development is not all that much about coding, but often is about figuring things out, getting in a mood to solving a small problem after the other. Sometimes is coded related, sometimes is just doing research about tooling, sometimes is fiddling around with configuration files.

So to get better you need to cultivate your creative process to solve problems one piece at the time.
